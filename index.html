Planning

Planning can extend outside the marked days. Find sponsors and people who want to come teach a workshop (days marked in green). Decide what is happening during each workshop. Find the best place on campus to conduct these workshops. Confirm venue for CTF. Write and test CTF flags.

Student Outreach

Spring semester starts February 19. Gather interested students. Visit CS/CIS classes, post fliers, etc.

CTF Workshop Days

CTF
